About Us

Welcome to LGBTQ Resources Kenya, where being you is celebrated, loud and proud! Dive into a world where support meets style, advocacy meets awesome, and resources are as vibrant as our community.

Our Mission

To empower LGBTQ individuals across Kenya with vital resources, foster community connections, and amplify voices for equality and acceptance.

Objectives

  • Provide access to essential health services and legal support tailored to the LGBTQ community.
  • Promote advocacy and awareness for LGBTQ rights and issues in Kenya.
  • Build a supportive community where LGBTQ individuals can connect, share experiences, and find solidarity.

Values

  • Celebrating diversity within the LGBTQ community.
  • Upholding inclusivity and equality in all our endeavors.
  • Providing accurate and up-to-date information to empower LGBTQ individuals in Kenya.

Ready to make waves? Whether you’re here to find help, make friends, or learn more, you’re in the right place. Let’s shine a rainbow spotlight on what matters most!
